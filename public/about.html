About TimeBank

TimeBank is a community-driven platform where people exchange skills and services through time credits instead of money. Our mission is to create stronger, more connected communities where everyone’s time is valued equally.

Our Mission & Vision

We believe in the power of community. By exchanging skills — whether tutoring, gardening, coding, or caregiving — members contribute to a network of shared resources.

Our vision is to build a society where time is the true currency, ensuring inclusivity, fairness, and empowerment for all.
